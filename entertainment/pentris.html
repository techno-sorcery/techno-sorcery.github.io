A Tetris clone, with pentominos instead of tetrominos.

Pentris


Controls (includes vim bindings)
Left, h: Move left
Right, l: Move right
Up, k: Rotate left
z, K: Rotate right
Down, j: Soft drop
Space: Hard drop
Enter: Hold use/store
